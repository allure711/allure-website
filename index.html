Allure Bar & Lounge was created for Washington, DC professionals looking for a place that feels both upscale and welcoming. A lounge where you can unwind after work, enjoy quality drinks and food, and stay late into the night without the pressure of a nightclub scene.

Our Philosophy

  • Quality drinks made right
