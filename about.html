About Allure Bar & Lounge

Located in Washington, DC, Allure Bar & Lounge was created to offer a premium nightlife experience where modern comfort meets upscale energy.

Our space blends warm lighting, stylish seating, curated music, and expertly crafted cocktails to create an atmosphere designed for professionals, creatives, and nightlife enthusiasts who appreciate elevated vibes.

From Free Hookah Monday and Taco Tuesday to our high-energy Allure Friday and Saturday nights, every evening is designed to deliver a memorable experience.

Whether you’re joining us for happy hour, a private celebration, or weekend nightlife, Allure provides the perfect balance of comfort, sophistication, and energy.
