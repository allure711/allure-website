About Allure

Where Washington, DC unwinds in style.

Our Story

Allure Bar & Lounge was created to deliver a refined nightlife experience in the heart of Washington, DC. We blend modern design, premium cocktails, curated music, and elevated comfort food to create a space built for professionals and nightlife enthusiasts who value atmosphere.

From weekday happy hours to high-energy weekend nights, every detail is designed to feel intentional — lighting, seating, music, and service all working together to create an upscale yet welcoming environment.

Whether you’re networking, celebrating, or simply unwinding after a long day, Allure offers the perfect balance of sophistication and energy.
